About Us

I am a NYC high school student and Sunset Park resident seeking to bring awareness to the cause of displacement and higher rents in Sunset Park. Gentrification is a process that affects most neighborhoods, since cities seek to improve neighborhoods which in turn raises property values. Some may introduce rezoning plans that supposedly seek to benefit Sunset Park, but in reality do not, like Industry City’s rezoning. Almost half of Sunset Park residents are struggling to pay rent, and many long time residents have been displaced, which could be prevented with a bit of education on what is happening, why it’s happening, and what can be done to prevent it.
