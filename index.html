Mission Statement:

In order to make change, people need to be aware of why that change is necessary. Many Sunset Park residents either do not know about Gentrification, or aren't sure how they would be affected by it. To put it simply, Gentrification in Sunset Park has resulted in over 50% of residents struggling to pay rent, and many people being displaced from their homes.
